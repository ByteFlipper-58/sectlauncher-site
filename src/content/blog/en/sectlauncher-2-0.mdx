SectLauncher 2.0 is a new chapter for the project. We decided not to release the previous version and instead completely rebuilt the launcher from scratch on a new engine, fixing old issues and building a solid foundation.

## What's Changed

### Transition to Rust and Tauri
We used to use Compose Multiplatform, but now we've completely switched to **Rust + Tauri v2**.
- The launcher is now much lighter.
- The interface works faster and smoother, like a regular system application.
- You no longer need a heavy Java (JVM) just to display windows and buttons.

### Forge Support
We’ve added **Forge** support. Now the launcher works correctly not only with Fabric but also with all popular mods and heavy Forge-based modpacks.

### Discord Integration
SectLauncher now supports **Discord Rich Presence**. Your game activity will automatically be displayed in your Discord profile — your friends will see that you are in the game.

### Microsoft Authorization
We’ve developed our own plugin for **Microsoft** account authorization. This allows you to log in to any official servers without issues and play with your usual skin and progress.

### Design and Themes
We've updated the look of the launcher, making it more calm. Most importantly — now you decide how it looks:
- You can choose a ready-made theme or set your own colors.
- You can put any of your own images on the home screen background.
